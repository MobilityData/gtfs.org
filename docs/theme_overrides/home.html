More than just a static site

Why GTFS?

GTFS facilitates seamless data sharing in transit, enabling app integration and real-time updates. Its community-driven approach enhances accessibility and transparency in public transportation.

By standardizing data formats, GTFS fosters innovation and collaboration among transit agencies and developers. This promotes efficient service delivery and empowers users with accurate and up-to-date information.

Built-in search

Material for MkDocs makes your documentation instantly searchable with zero effort: say goodbye to costly third-party crawler-based solutions that can take hours to update. Ship your documentation with a highly customizable and blazing fast search running entirely in the user's browser at no extra cost.
